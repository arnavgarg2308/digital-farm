Broiler Chickens

Age (Weeks) Feed Type Quantity Instructions
0-2 weeks Chick Starter Ad-libitum (freely) DO: Use a fine crumble feed. DO NOT: Run out of feed or water, as this impacts growth.
2-4 weeks Grower Pellets Ad-libitum DO: Transition to a grower diet gradually over a few days. DO NOT: Feed whole grains at this stage.
4+ weeks Finisher Pellets Ad-libitum DO: Reduce protein slightly. DO NOT: Feed raw or spoiled food.

Laying Hens

Time Feed Type Quantity (per hen) Instructions
Morning Layer Mash/Pellets 110 - 120 g DO: Provide most of the daily feed in the morning. DO NOT: Feed kitchen scraps that are high in salt or sugar.
Afternoon Grit or Oyster Shells As needed DO: Offer separately to aid digestion and produce strong eggshells.
Evening Scratch Grains Small amount DO: Feed as a treat to keep them warm overnight. DO NOT: Overfeed grains, as it can reduce egg production.
